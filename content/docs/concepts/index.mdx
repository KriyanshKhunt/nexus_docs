Before diving into features and APIs, these concepts explain **how Nexus Signal is designed** and why it behaves the way it does.

## Core principles

1. **Asynchronous by default** — triggers return in milliseconds; workers handle providers
2. **BYOP** — your keys, your bills, your data
3. **Multi-tenant isolation** — organizations, environments, and encrypted provider credentials
4. **Observable delivery** — every step logged from ingestion to carrier response
