Deliver notifications directly to team chat tools.

## Org-level setup

Configure incoming webhook URLs in **Integrations** for each platform. All subscribers share the org webhook unless overridden.

## Per-subscriber routing

Pass webhook URLs on trigger recipients for user-specific channels (e.g. personal Slack DM webhook):

```ts
recipients: [
  {
    externalId: 'user_1',
    slackWebhookUrl: 'https://hooks.slack.com/services/T…/B…/…',
    discordWebhookUrl: 'https://discord.com/api/webhooks/…',
    teamsWebhookUrl: 'https://outlook.office.com/webhook/…',
  },
]
```

Subscriber URLs **override** org credentials at dispatch time. URLs persist on the subscriber profile for future triggers.

## Payload formats

| Channel | Format |
|---------|--------|
| Slack | `{ "text": "…" }` |
| Discord | `{ "content": "…" }` |
| Teams | Adaptive Card with body text |

Templates support `slack`, `discord`, and `ms_teams` content fields with i18n variants.

## Workflow canvas

Add **Slack**, **Discord**, or **Teams** channel nodes to your workflow step tree.
